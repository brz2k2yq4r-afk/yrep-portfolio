About Me

The Person Behind the Edit

I'm Perry — a passionate video editor who goes by YREP. What started as pure love for storytelling through footage has grown into a real craft. I live for the moment a sequence clicks, when the music hits right with the cut and suddenly something ordinary becomes unforgettable.

Every project I take on gets my full creative energy. Whether it's a music video, a short film, a social media reel, or a brand story — I treat it like it matters. Because it does.
